# Prepare
To prepare for annotating anything in CoralNet, you need access to the CoralNet source.


## Register
If you don't already have an account on CoralNet, you need to register first:

1.  Go to the [CoralNet registration page](https://coralnet.ucsd.edu/accounts/register/).
2.  Fill out the information and click _Register_.
3.  Once registration is confirmed, you receive an activation email. Click the activation link to activate your account.


## Obtaining an invite
We are using a single CoralNet source in which we annotate all the photos from the different groups. To obtain an invite to access this group:

1.  Send me an email with _your CoralNet username_ at [d.a.a.pelsmaeker@tudelft.nl](mailto:d.a.a.pelsmaeker@tudelft.nl).
2.  I will send you an invite from the CoralNet website.
3.  Find all pending invites on the [CoralNet invites page](https://coralnet.ucsd.edu/source/invites/) and accept the invite.
