# Annotate
This guide details how to annotate your [imported](../1_import/index.md) photos in [CoralNet](https://coralnet.ucsd.edu).


## Open annotation tool
1.  Go to the _Images_ page.
2.  Click on the image to annotate to open it.
3.  Click the _Annotation tool_ button to open the annotation tool.


## Annotation tool
The annotation tool shows the photo with a grid of points overlaid on it. The goal is to have each point annotated with the label of what is at this point in the photo. You can either confirm the machine-annotated point or pick your own label.

Points are colored based on their status: unannotated, suggested (yellow), annotated (purple), selected (green). The table on the right shows the current label for each point. The label text is colored based on their status: suggested (grey), annotated (black).

!!! note ""
    Suggested labels are not counted in the statistics until you confirm the label (making the point annotated). Once all points are annotated and saved, the text `ALL DONE` will appear under the table.


### Zoom the image
To zoom the image:

-  Zoom in: left-click on the image; or click the :material-magnify-plus: button; or press ++shift+up++.
-  Zoom out: right-click on the image; or click the :material-magnify-minus: button; or press ++shift+down++.


### Select points
There are multiple ways to select points to annotate:

-  Select points by clicking the point number in the table on the right. You can select multiple points.
-  Hold ++ctrl++ (++cmd++ on Mac) and click a point in the image. You can select multiple points.
-  Select a point by clicking the label text in the table on the right. You can select one point.
-  Hold ++shift++ and click a point in the image. You can select one point.
-  Press the ++period++ key to select the first unannotated point.
-  Press ++tab++ to go to the next point (annotated or unannotated).
-  Press ++shift+tab++ to go to the previous point.
-  Click the :material-numeric-0-box: button to unselect all points.
-  Click the :material-plus-box: button to select all unannotated points.
-  Click the :material-arrow-up-down: button to invert your current selection (unselect all selected points while selecting all unselected points).
-  Click the :material-select: button and drag a rectangle on the photo to add those points to your selection.


### Pick a label
Either click the label in the label grid below the image, or start typing in the floating textbox on the image. Machine-generated suggestions will appear ranked by confidence. To pick the top-most suggestion, simply press ++enter++. To pick another label, start typing its code and press ++enter++ once the correct label is selected.


### Save your work
Either click the _Save Progress_ button under the table, or press ++ctrl+s++ (++cmd+s++ on Mac).


### Move to another photo
To move to the next photo, click the _Next_ link or press ++g++ then ++n++.
To move to the previous photo, click the _Prev_ link or press ++g++ then ++p++.
